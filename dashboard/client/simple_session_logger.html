GoldenForm Session Logger

Instructions

1. Press EN button on ESP32 to START logging

2. Press BOOT button on ESP32 to STOP logging

3. LED on ESP32 shows logging status (ON=logging, OFF=stopped)

4. Data automatically transfers to laptop after stopping

5. Visualization shows realistic forearm rotation

IMU Location: Right under the elbow on forearm
