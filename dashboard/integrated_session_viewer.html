Calibration Guide

Your wearable has a motion sensor (IMU) that needs to learn its surroundings before it can accurately track your strokes. This is called calibration. You only need to do this once — the device remembers it after all values reach 3/3.

Before you start

  • Make sure you are connected to the wearable's Wi-Fi network (it creates its own hotspot named GoldenForm_XXXX).
  • The calibration badges on this page update in real time — watch them go from 0/3 to 3/3 as you move.
  • Work on a desk or table, away from metal, magnets, and electronics.

Step-by-step (follow this order)

1
Gyroscope (Gyro)

Place the wearable flat on a table and leave it completely still for about 5 seconds. Don't touch the table — even small vibrations can reset progress. You should see Gyro jump to 3/3 quickly.

2
Accelerometer (Accel)

Pick up the wearable and slowly tilt it to rest on each of its 6 faces — think of it like a dice: top, bottom, left side, right side, front, back. Hold each position steady for 2–3 seconds before moving to the next. No need to rush.

3
Magnetometer (Mag)

Hold the wearable in front of you and draw a large figure-8 (infinity sign) in the air, about arm's length wide. Move smoothly and keep going for 10–15 seconds. Stay away from laptops, phones, metal furniture, and speakers — they interfere with the magnetic reading.

4
System (Sys)

This one fuses all three sensors together. Slowly rotate the wearable in random directions — imagine stirring a pot, then tilting the pot side to side, then flipping it. Mix pitch (nodding), roll (tilting), and yaw (turning). Smooth, deliberate movements for about 10 seconds. This is usually the last to lock in.

When you're done

Once all four badges show 3/3, the card will say "Good to swim" and the device permanently saves the calibration. You can disconnect from Wi-Fi and start logging your swim. If you power-cycle the device before all four reach 3/3, you'll need to calibrate again.

Troubleshooting

  • Values stuck at 0/3? Make sure you're connected to the wearable's Wi-Fi. Refresh the page if badges aren't updating.
  • Mag won't reach 3/3? Move further from metal and electronics. Try a different room. The figure-8 motion should be wide and slow.
  • System keeps dropping? This is normal — the System register is sensitive during motion. Keep doing slow rotations and it will lock in.
  • Lost calibration after restart? The device only saves permanently once all four reach 3/3 in the same session. Try again from Step 1.
