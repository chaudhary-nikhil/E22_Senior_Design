Getting Started

1. Put on your GoldenForm device and press the button to start recording.

2. Swim your session, then press the button again to stop.

3. Hold the button to enter sync mode — the device creates a WiFi network.

4. Connect your phone/laptop to the GoldenForm WiFi, then click Sync from Device below.

5. For multi-device: repeat with your second device, then Merge to combine.
